With the advance of technology, it has made it easier and quicker for users to browse through information. Now a days, it has become a habit for users to scroll through an unlimited source of information, such as Reddit, only to briefly pause and to quickly move on to the next information.

Matter of f-actually is a curated experience of "outrageous" facts (although very limited), disrupting the user behaviour of reading and collecting information. Through simple animations and interactions, words and context are manipulated to make the user think critically, reconsider and be aware of readily available information and the consequences of mindless actions.

So what was the reward? Well, that's up to you to decide what you've gotten out of this brief experience.
